## General Rules

1. **Be respectful towards everyone** no matter race, sexuality, and or beliefs
2. **No harassment, hate speech or discrimination** of any kind will be tolerated.
3. **Continuous hate or targeted attacks,** including jokes that go too far, are prohibited.
4. **Actions influenced by monetary bribes** or similar offers are **strictly forbidden.**
5. **Community moderators are not Comfora Employees,** so don't treat them as employees as they are volunteers from the community.
    - Community moderators may be given **read-only or restricted access** to internal Comfora SSO enabled services in order to assist you as
    much as they can, they reserve the right to notify a Comfora employee for further inquires.
6. **Comfora is a English Only** Discord server to improve conversations, planned inclusions for more languages are in motion but for current day, it's english only.

### Content and Discussions

1. **No NSFW Content:** Avoid posting or sharing any NSFW or obscene material.
2. **Respect for Opinions:** Allow space for diverse opinions; avoid escalating arguments or making personal attacks.
3. **Political and Sensitive Topics:** Keep political and highly sensitive discussions out of the main channels.

### Community Conduct

1. **No Spam or Unapproved Promotions:** Do not spam or promote content without staff approval.
2. **No Threats:** Threatening or harmful behavior is strictly prohibited.
3. **Conflict Resolution:** Work to defuse conflicts rather than exacerbating them.

### Channel Use and Etiquette

1. **Stay On Topic:** Use channels for their designated purposes. Move off-topic conversations to appropriate channels.
2. **Maintain Quality Conversations:** Encourage meaningful discussions and avoid repetitive or trivial chatter.

### Administration and Moderation

1. **Follow Discord TOS:** Adhere to all Discord Terms of Service guidelines.
2. **Moderator Authority:** Decisions made by moderators or higher staff members are final. Repeated attempts to counter or dispute these decisions are grounds for a ban.
3. **Reporting and Support:** Use designated channels (e.g., `#tickets`) for moderation-related queries and support needs.

### Integrity and Fair Play

1. **No Bribery:** Decisions should never be influenced by bribes or external incentives.
2. **No Piracy or Illegal Activities:** Discussions or actions related to piracy or illegal activities are forbidden.

### Additional Rules

1. **Avoid Excessive Special Characters in Nicknames:** Use easily readable names without impersonating public figures.
2. **Respect Privacy:** Do not discuss or share details about punishments or private conversations without consent.
3. **No Problematic Content:** Avoid sharing content that could induce epilepsy, crash the Discord client, or trigger antivirus warnings.

### Enforcing and Adapting Rules
- **Regular Review:** Continuously review and adapt the rules to ensure a positive and inclusive community environment.
- **Member Feedback:** Encourage members to provide feedback on rules and community practices to foster an open and evolving community.
- **AI Suggestions:** Using in-house local internal AI to suggest how we can improve our rules and guidelines for a better community.
